ASIAN STREET BY THAI

The restaurant prides itself on dishes made with homemade sauces, delivering an experience reminiscent of Thailand's vibrant street food scene. Favorites among patrons include Pad Thai, papaya salad, and glazed duck with rice. The warm hospitality and affordable prices make it a popular spot for both quick lunches and relaxed dinners. ​ Tripadvisor JLT Dining Operating daily from 11:00 AM to 10:30 PM, Asian Street by Thai offers dine-in, takeaway, and delivery options. For those seeking an unpretentious setting with authentic Thai cuisine, this establishment is a must-visit in Dubai.
